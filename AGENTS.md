AGENTS policy
=============

for without any AI means for years since creation, and for once and forever
(the author thinks nowadays) will be kept free from AI content throughout, no
matter its quality, provided generated stuff is recognized.

Exception to this: score-editors/, sub-directories in it are excluded from version control. Please cf. AGENTS.md therein.
