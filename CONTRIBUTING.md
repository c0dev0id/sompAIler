CONTRIBUTING
============

AI-less humans
--------------

In issues, please describe concisely:
- Operational base: operation system, docker image, versions and other stuff
  worth knowing.
- What occurs or looks like it shouldn't be that way
- What is expected.
- Related score-editor project (link if public), if applicable

Merge-requests and pull-requests are welcome by anyone able not to follow LLM
conventions and who use individual (but understandable) English. Cues of LLM
use I recognize can lead to ignorance / close without comment.


People with AI-support/bot-aid
------------------------------

Please see score-editors/AGENTS.md. Participants to the challenge are welcome.
The upstream project sticks to the textarea for nerds to fill in by heart,
sheet music interpretation and musical imagination. Or by AI means. Or by
AI-generated Flask blueprints you guys create. The first three projects are mentioned in the README file under "Related score editors". They are ranked for freshness and approved quality.

Please do not post AI-generated issue reports (s.a.) without approval by the
critical eye and human final touch. If it looks perfect from the start and does
not need some, do *not* post, my opinion could damage its aura. If related to a
score-editor blueprint project, please link to it for an uprank in the waiting
queue of upstream support.
